ChangePaces.com

An artful web game for everyone to play.

1.) Turn on your volume

2.) Leave your browser window on and active.

3.) When you hear "Change paces" you change the speed at which you exist.

4.) Your screen will indicate the appropriate speed you are to utilize to calibrate your body and mind.

Here are a few to get you started: Ultra slow, bumble, even keel, pitter-patter, Godspeed. The estimated times are not accurate, but do serve to provide another altering factor to your understanding of time.
